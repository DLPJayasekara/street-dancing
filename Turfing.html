Turfing

Turfing, also known as "Turf Dance," is a street dance style that originated in Oakland, California, in the early 2000s. The term "turf" stands for "Taking Up Room on the Floor." Turfing is characterized by its intricate footwork, creative storytelling, and close connection to the local community and culture of Oakland.

Key elements of turfing include:

  1. Flexing and Gliding: Turfing incorporates flexing movements, where dancers showcase their flexibility by contorting their bodies into various shapes and positions. Gliding, or "turfing on air," is another hallmark of the style, where dancers appear to glide or float across the floor with smooth and fluid movements.
  2. Turf Battles: Turfing often involves friendly competition and battles between dancers or dance crews. During turf battles, dancers showcase their skills, creativity, and improvisational abilities, responding to each other's movements in real-time and engaging in playful banter.
  3. Storytelling and Narrative: Turfing is known for its emphasis on storytelling and narrative. Dancers use their movements to convey stories, themes, and emotions, often drawing inspiration from their personal experiences, struggles, and triumphs.
  4. Turfing Styles and Variations: Turfing encompasses a variety of styles and variations, each with its own unique movements, techniques, and characteristics. Some common styles within turfing include boogaloo, popping, waving, and tutting, which are often blended together in creative and innovative ways.
  5. Community and Culture: Turfing is deeply rooted in the local community and culture of Oakland, with dancers often representing their neighborhoods and expressing pride in their roots. Turfing events and gatherings serve as important social hubs where dancers come together to share their passion for dance and celebrate their shared identity.
  6. Innovation and Evolution: Turfing is a dynamic and ever-evolving dance style, with dancers continually pushing the boundaries of creativity and innovation. New movements, techniques, and styles are constantly being developed, reflecting the diverse influences and artistic expressions within the turfing community.

Overall, turfing is a vibrant and expressive street dance style that celebrates individuality, creativity, and community. It serves as a platform for self-expression, empowerment, and cultural exchange, while also preserving the rich heritage and traditions of Oakland's dance culture.
